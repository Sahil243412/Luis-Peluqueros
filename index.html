About Us

Where Art Meets Hair Craft

Founded in the heart of Madrid in 1998, Luis Peluqueros has grown from a passionate dream into one of the city's most celebrated hair salons.

Our philosophy is simple: every client is unique. Led by master stylist Luis and a team of highly trained professionals, we combine timeless artistry with the latest techniques to deliver transformative results.

From precision cuts to bespoke color work, luxurious treatments to bridal styling — we believe your hair tells your story, and we're here to help you tell it beautifully.
